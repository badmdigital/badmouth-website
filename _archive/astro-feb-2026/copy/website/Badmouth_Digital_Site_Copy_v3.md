**CAMPAIGN CATEGORY: GET PAST CUSTOMERS BACK**

**Reactivation Campaign**

Target: Past customers who haven't heard from you in 6--36 months.

Angle: "You used us before. It's probably time again."

Email + SMS. Reminder with a soft reason to come back. Simple booking
link. Lowest trust barrier, highest ROI per dollar. This is always the
first campaign we recommend.

**Seasonal Push**

Target: Your full customer and lead database.

Angle: "This gets worse in \[season\]."

Pre-winter inspections. Pre-summer AC tune-ups. Spring gutter cleanouts.
Whatever fits your trade. Not fake urgency. Real consequences tied to
real timing.

**Referral Campaign**

Target: Happy customers (especially ones who left reviews).

Angle: "Know someone dealing with this?"

Your review system already identifies satisfied customers. This campaign
adds a referral ask right after the positive review. Turns satisfaction
into pipeline. Two steps: get the review, then ask for the referral.
Simple. Effective.

**CAMPAIGN CATEGORY: RECOVER LOST REVENUE**

**Abandoned Estimate Follow-Up**

Target: People who got quotes but never moved forward.

Angle: "Still want to move forward?"

3-touch sequence. Handles the common objections. Clarifies scope. This
alone often recovers 10--20% of stuck revenue. One of our electrician
clients pulled \$18,000 in 90 days just from this campaign.

**Dormant Lead Re-Qualification**

Target: Leads older than 90 days that never converted.

Angle: "Still dealing with this?"

Simple sequence that segments your old leads: still need help, already
fixed it, or no longer interested. Cleans your list and recovers revenue
at the same time. The ones who respond "yes" are the warmest leads
you'll ever get.

**Win-Back Campaign**

Target: People who got a quote from you but went with someone else.

Angle: "How'd that work out?"

Aggressive but honest. These people already know you. If their first
choice didn't work out, you're the natural second call. This campaign
catches them at the moment of regret and gives them an easy way back.

**CAMPAIGN CATEGORY: GROW WHAT YOU'VE GOT**

**Revenue Expansion Campaign**

Target: Existing customers or recent jobs.

Angle: "If you did X, you probably need Y."

Two triggers: within 30--60 days for add-ons ("Add this while we're
already there") and 3--6 months out for upgrades. Increases average
ticket size without a dollar spent on new customer acquisition.

**Quick-Fix Entry Offer**

Target: Cold or warm list. People who haven't committed yet.

Angle: A small, low-friction service at a clear price point.

Example: \$99 inspection. \$49 diagnostic. The goal isn't profit on the
offer. It's getting in the door. Works best when your average job value

**Important:**
